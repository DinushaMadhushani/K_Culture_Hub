HANGUL

Korean Alphabet

Hangul is the Korean alphabet, created by King Sejong in 1443. It's considered one of the most scientific writing systems in the world.

Did You Know?

Hangul was designed to be easy to learn. The shapes of the consonants mimic the shape of the mouth when pronouncing them.

You can learn to read Hangul in just a few hours. Many learners report being able to read Korean script after a single day of study!
