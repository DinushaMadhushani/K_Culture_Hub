Why We Started

KoreaVerse was born from a simple idea: to create a comprehensive, accessible platform for anyone interested in Korean culture. As Korean culture gained global popularity, we noticed a lack of centralized, authentic resources for those wanting to explore beyond K-Pop and K-Dramas.

Our founder, Min-Ji Park, envisioned a space where traditional heritage and modern innovations coexist, offering a balanced view of Korea's rich cultural landscape.
