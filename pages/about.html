Our Story

Discover the journey of Dancheong Diary and our passion for blending Korean heritage with contemporary fashion

Where Tradition Meets Modernity

Founded in 2018, Dancheong Diary began as a passion project by designer Min-ji Park. Growing up in Seoul surrounded by the vibrant colors of Gyeongbokgung Palace and the elegant lines of traditional Hanbok, Min-ji felt a deep connection to Korea's artistic heritage.

After studying fashion design in Paris, Min-ji returned to Seoul with a vision: to create clothing that honors traditional Korean aesthetics while embracing contemporary silhouettes. What started as a small studio in Insadong has grown into a beloved brand that celebrates Korea's rich cultural legacy through fashion.

The name "Dancheong Diary" comes from the traditional Korean decorative coloring used on wooden buildings. Just as Dancheong artists documented stories through their vibrant patterns, we record our cultural journey through each collection we create.

Our Mission & Values

At Dancheong Diary, we believe fashion should tell a story. Our mission is to preserve Korean cultural heritage through contemporary design, creating pieces that connect wearers to centuries of artistic tradition while expressing modern individuality.

Cultural Preservation

We research and honor traditional techniques, ensuring they remain relevant for future generations.

Sustainable Practices

From sourcing to production, we prioritize eco-friendly materials and ethical manufacturing.

Innovative Design

We reinterpret traditional elements in fresh, contemporary ways that resonate globally.

Community Connection

We collaborate with Korean artisans and support cultural preservation initiatives.
